Welcome to my personal site! My name is Sukolsak Sakshuwong, or สุคลศักดิ์ ศักดิ์ชูวงษ์. I am a fifth-year PhD student in Management Science and Engineering at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Ashish Goel. I was selected as a representative of Thailand for the International Olympiad in Informatics in 2005 and received a full ride scholarship from the Royal Thai Government to study in the United States.

My research interests include system design and human-computer interaction. I am excited about applying computer science to real-world problems, such as kidney matching and computational genomics.
